About

About SNU ad settings

This ad engine gives you complete control over what ads you want to see on SNU. You have the freedom to choose any ad you want, block and keep ads all the way down to the keywords said in the ad, or the objects in the ad.

You can control their location, their topic, their owner, their product, and EVERYTHING about them. This is a lot of power to give.

To keep it fair, however you will always need to keep at least 5 ads enabled.

This system should also push advertisers to make better, and more diverse advertisements that the consumers (you) will enjoy.

Take a look around these settings and feel the power of control at your fingertips
